# Customer-Behavior-Dashboard
**Project Overview**

This project analyzes retail customer transaction data to generate meaningful business insights about purchasing behavior, revenue distribution, product performance, discount effectiveness, and customer loyalty.
The objective was to understand how different customer segments contribute to revenue and how factors such as gender, subscription status, shipping type, discounts, and previous purchases impact overall sales performance.
The analysis was performed using MySQL for data querying and Microsoft Power BI for visualization and dashboard creation.

**Problem Statement**

The business wanted to:
Compare total revenue generated by male vs female customers
Identify customers who used discounts but still spent above average
Determine the top 5 highest-rated products
Compare spending behavior between Standard and Express shipping
Analyze whether subscribed customers spend more
Measure which products rely most on discounts
Segment customers into New, Returning, and Loyal
Identify top 3 most purchased products within each category
Analyze whether repeat buyers are likely to subscribe
Measure revenue contribution by age group

**Analysis Performed**

1. Revenue Analysis by Gender
-Calculated total revenue contribution of male and female customers to understand demographic impact on sales.

2. High-Value Discount Customers
-Identified customers who applied discounts but still spent more than the overall average purchase amount.

3. Product Rating Analysis
-Determined the top 5 products with the highest average review ratings to evaluate product satisfaction.

4. Shipping Type Comparison
-Compared average purchase amounts between Standard and Express shipping methods.

5. Subscription Impact on Revenue
-Analyzed total customers, average spend, and total revenue for subscribers vs non-subscribers.

6. Discount Dependency by Product
-Measured the percentage of purchases made using discounts for each product and identified the top 5 most discount-driven products.

7. Customer Segmentation
-Segmented customers into:
 New (1 previous purchase)
 Returning (2–10 previous purchases)
 Loyal (More than 10 previous purchases)
 Then calculated the count of customers in each segment.

8. Category-wise Product Performance
-Identified the top 3 most purchased products within each product category using ranking techniques.

9. Repeat Buyer Subscription Analysis
-Analyzed whether customers with more than 5 previous purchases are likely to be subscribed members.

10. Revenue by Age Group
-Calculated total revenue contribution of each age group to identify the most valuable customer segments.

**Key Business Insights**

Revenue distribution varies significantly across gender groups.
Some customers spend heavily even when discounts are applied.
A small number of products consistently receive higher ratings.
Shipping method influences average purchase value.
Subscribers generally contribute higher total revenue.
Certain products depend heavily on discounts to drive sales.
Loyal customers form a strong revenue base.
Product demand differs across categories.
Repeat buyers show strong subscription trends.
Specific age groups contribute more to overall revenue.

**Business Impact**

Helps optimize discount strategy
Supports targeted marketing campaigns
Improves customer retention planning
Identifies high-performing products and categories
Assists in subscription growth strategies
Enables data-driven decision making
